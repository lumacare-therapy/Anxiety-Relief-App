Common Questions

Everything you're wondering about anxiety relief

How quickly do these techniques work? +
Many people feel relief within 2-3 minutes of breathing exercises. Grounding techniques work almost instantly for panic.
Do I need to create an account? +
Nope. Everything is completely free with no login required. We believe mental health tools should be accessible to everyone.
Can this replace therapy? +
These are tools for daily support, not a replacement for professional help. If you're struggling deeply, please reach out to a therapist.
What if I'm having a panic attack right now? +
Try 5-4-3-2-1 grounding: Name 5 things you see, 4 you feel, 3 you hear, 2 you smell, 1 you taste. Then try 4-7-8 breathing. You're safe.
